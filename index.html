About This Event

What does successful collaboration between pharma and advocacy actually look like? This conversation explores the Sanofi × MTS Sickle Cell Foundation partnership as a real-world example — the origin story, how both sides defined success, and the outcomes that followed.

Whether you work in pharma, advocacy, clinical research, or community health, this session offers clear best practices and proof that trust-based collaboration improves outcomes for patients and sponsors alike.

  • 01 The origin story — what shared need created momentum to collaborate
  • 02 Defining success together — aligning on what "working" looks like for both organizations
  • 03 Mutual value — what each side gained from the partnership
  • 04 Impact and outcomes — meaningful results and what comes next
